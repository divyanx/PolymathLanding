Account Deletion

Polymath does not require a separate account. Your data lives on your devices and optionally in your iCloud.

Delete local data

  1. Open Polymath.
  2. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy.
  3. Select “Clear Local Data.”

Delete iCloud data

  1. Open system Settings on your device.
  2. Go to your Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.
  3. Find Polymath and delete its iCloud data.

Need help?

Email support@polymaths.life if you need assistance removing data.
